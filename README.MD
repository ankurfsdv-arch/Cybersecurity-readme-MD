## 💡 Tips for Success

- **Build your lab first** — Never practice on real systems without authorization.
- **Document everything** — Write notes and reports after every lab session.
- **Practice on platforms** — TryHackMe, HackTheBox, and PortSwigger are your best friends.
- **Learn one tool deeply** — Master Nmap, Burp Suite, and Metasploit before moving on.
- **Follow CVEs** — Stay updated at [cve.mitre.org](https://cve.mitre.org/) and [exploit-db.com](https://www.exploit-db.com/).
- **Join communities** — Reddit [r/netsec](https://www.reddit.com/r/netsec/), [r/hacking](https://www.reddit.com/r/hacking/), TCM Security Discord.
- **Aim for OSCP** — It is the gold standard for penetration testers.
